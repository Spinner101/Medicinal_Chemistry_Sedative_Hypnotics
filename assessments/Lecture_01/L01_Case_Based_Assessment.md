# Lecture 01 — Case-Based Assessment

## Case 1
A 76-year-old with cirrhosis and insomnia requires short-term therapy.

**Best choice:** Temazepam  
**Justification:** 3-OH → glucuronidation → minimal accumulation

---

## Case 2
Which benzodiazepine should be avoided in elderly insomnia?

**Answer:** Flurazepam  
**Reason:** Long-acting active metabolites
